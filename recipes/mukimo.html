How to make Mukimo

Description

Mukimo is easily one of the sweetest and healthiest dishes you'll ever make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups soft maize
  • 2 cups soft beans
  • 1kg potatoes
  • 10 pumpkin leaves
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Mix and soak two cups of soft maize and beans (preferably yellow beans) for ten hours
  2. Boil this mixture till cooked
  3. In a separate pot steam the potatoes and pumpkin leaves till soft and mashable
  4. Mix the two and mash the potatoes and leaves completely
  5. In a separate pot cook a large onion and garlic in three spoons of cooking oil till brown, add the mukimo, salt to flavour, and stir for about three minutes
  6. Your mukimo is now ready to eat
